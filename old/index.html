          Welcome!

I  paint ceramic house plaques with names, addresses,  or numbers in colors and designs  personalized for your home!  My name is April Frost.
House Plaque Examples
    House plaques are made from Earthenware clay, in 2 sizes.  Lettering is done only on the large size.  April hand paints and letters (or numbers) each plaque.

    Plaques can be horizontal or vertical.  You choose the design and the colors.  Allow 4-6 weeks for painting and firing and 2-10 days, depending on your location, for shipping.
