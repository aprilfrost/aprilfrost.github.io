Ordering a Ceramic Address Plaque

  1. Choose the design you want from the Plaque Gallery, or e-mail April to discuss your idea — you might want a "special order" of a place or pet.
  2. Decide what you want on your plaque. Name? Number only? Full street address? Think about colors as well.
    Lettering on LARGE size only, smaller plaques are NUMBERS only!
  3. Email or call April to make your order.

*Orders will not be painted until paid in full. If you decide not to purchase a plaque after working with April and having drawings done, you will be charged $10 design fee only. Plaques run from $80 to $120 (and about $60 for smaller, numbers only, plaques) plus about $10 shipping, depending on the complexity of the design you request. Plaques have 2 holes for hanging and come with 2 -1 1/2 inch brass screws and 2 washers.
