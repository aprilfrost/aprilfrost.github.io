Other Art Projects

Along with making ceramic house plaques, I enjoy doing art in other mediums. I create large and small scale mosaic and tile projects, painting, and fabric projects. Lately I have played with felting and monoprinting on clay. I thought it would be fun to include a page of mixed media projects. Please feel free to e-mail if you see something here you think might be up your alley!
